Mission & Governance

The Pleroma Archive is an independent, non-political library-church dedicated to the neutral stewardship and preservation of Gnostic records. Our mandate is the protection of the documentary heritage of Gnosis, ensuring that ancient and modern insights are never lost to time or institutional censorship.

We do not preach, nor do we evangelise. We exist solely to preserve and provide public access to the records of the Spark.
